WELCOME TO HEX


Welcome to MacGregor’s H Entry - also known as Hex! HEX has a historically distinct, nerdy, DIY, rebellious culture, situated in the southwest corner of the MacG Lowrise. We are the classy-ass folk who cook a lot of fancy-ass dinners. We're the outspoken, vocal group that isn't afraid to do crazy things and get in "trouble" with various MIT admins. We are those nocturnal assholes who are awake when you're asleep, but are also the sleepy assholes who aren't afraid tell you to shut the fuck up. We used hold the record for most fire alarms set off by any entry (hence our alter namesakes / door signs, the Fire Hazards) until a bunch of frosh in the high rise didn't realize they didn't know how to use stoves. Join Hex. Learn to cook.

While entry culture has changed and shifted over the years, we still enjoy building things, gaming, hacking, and pulling crazy stunts. If you're into East Side culture with a bit more cleanliness, a bit more serenity, and a bit more discreteness, this is the place for you.
